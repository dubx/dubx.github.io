About Us

Created by passionate developers who love music and messing around with computers. We started off on a similar website known as plug.dj (2012-2015) which we found to be quite amazing. Since it's closing, we decided to come to Dubtrack and attempt to make it as enjoyable as plug.dj was. We knew this would be a huge goal to climb, and nothing could replace plug.dj. However, we are determined and mainly want to have a good time with the people around us on the site.

user.js Extension Bookmarklet

You can install our extension on Chrome, Safari, Opera, Firefox and Microsoft Edge. To use our script, either click the Chrome Extension or User.js Extension button, or drag the Bookmarket button to your Bookmarks Bar.

What Makes Dub X Special

1

We are very responsive to our users and reply as soon as we can. If you have a question or bug report, you should hear back from us within a couple days max!

2

All of our features and functions are aimed at making Dubtrack.fm a fun and creative place. We try to give each function the availability to be turned off or on, depening if you like it. There's no forcing you into anything.

3

Customize! If you want to customize your Dubtrack experience, you definitely can with Dub X. We have the option to insert your own custom CSS file or custom background image into the site. All your settings are stored through your browser cache & local-storage, so don't worry about needing to re-set it over and over again.
